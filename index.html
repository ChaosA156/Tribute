About The Arbiter

He was a lead cause of the fall of the Covenant during the Human-Covenant War. He currently holds the title of Arbiter. The Arbiter is currently the leader of the Swords of Sanghelios and the Kaidon of Sanghelios.

  1. Led the greatest fleet ever known to both The Covenant and the UNSC
  2. Faced The Flood
  3. Silenced Truth
  4. Prevented a Halo Ring from activating
  5. Turned the tide of the Human-Covenant War
  6. Has epic quotes
  7. Started the Swords of Sangheilios after the fall of the Covenant
  8. Became the Kaidon of Sanghelios
  9. Ended the civil war plagueing his homeworld
  10. Crushed the last major part of the New Covenant's remaining forces

"I have faced The Flood and Silenced Truth. I shall quell your thunder and douse your flame."
